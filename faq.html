Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is EcomDeals?

EcomDeals is your one-stop platform to explore top-rated, trending products from trusted online stores like Amazon, Flipkart, and Meesho. We help you find great deals with minimal effort.

2. Do you sell these products?

No, we don’t sell any products directly. We simply showcase and link to products available on trusted platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, and Meesho.

3. Are your links affiliate links?

Yes, many of our links are affiliate links. This means we may earn a small commission if you make a purchase using them—at no extra cost to you. It helps us keep the site running.

4. How often are the product listings updated?

We update the product listings regularly to include the latest trends, best deals, and top-rated items. However, price and availability may change on the retailer’s website.

5. How do I know if a deal is still active?

Since deals can expire or change, always verify the product price and availability on the original website before completing your purchase.

6. Can I suggest a product to be listed?

Absolutely! If you know a great deal or product, feel free to contact us and we may consider featuring it on EcomDeals.

7. Is it safe to buy through your links?

Yes. All links redirect you to trusted platforms like Amazon, Flipkart, or Meesho. All transactions and data sharing happen on their secure websites.

8. Do you collect my personal data?

No, we don’t collect personal data. However, we may use cookies for analytics and to show relevant ads via services like Google AdSense. For more details, check our Privacy Policy.

9. Is EcomDeals available as an app?

Currently, EcomDeals is a web-based platform. A mobile app is in the works—stay tuned!

10. How can I contact you?

You can reach us anytime via our Contact Us page. We’d love to hear your suggestions or feedback.
