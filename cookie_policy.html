What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you browse websites. They are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

This helps us to improve the performance and design of our website. We may use third-party analytics tools that use these cookies on our behalf.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow our website to remember choices you make (such as your language preference) and provide enhanced, more personal features. They may also be used to provide services you have asked for.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. You can usually find these settings in the "Options," "Tools," or "Preferences" menu of your browser. You can configure your browser to block all cookies or to notify you when a cookie is being set. However, please note that blocking all cookies may affect your ability to access certain parts of our website.
